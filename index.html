What is AR?

Transform the world around you with AR.

Augmented Reality (AR) is a technology that allows you to see virtual objects in the real world, through your smartphone, tablet or AR headset. It's like a magic window that lets you interact with digital content in the real world.
AR shows virtual elements or objects in the real-world environment, creating a hybrid reality that enhances the user's experience. AR allows users to interact with virtual elements in real time, providing a more immersive and engaging experience. AR systems can use markers to track the position and position of the user.

What is VR?

Experience the impossible with VR technology.

Virtual Reality (VR) is a technology that creates a completely digital environment that allows you to experience a completely different world, created by a computer, through a headset that you wear on your head. It's like being inside a video game or movie, where you can look around and move like you're actually there.
Users can interact with objects in the virtual environment using various peripherals, such as gloves, controllers, or even body movements. A key component of VR is the head-mounted display (HMD), which is a device that covers the user's eyes and displays the virtual environment.
